About Us

An Organic Beginning

The Organic Italian got its start the way the best dinners do – thoughtfully, hungrily and with way too many people around the table. Created by Bella Italia, the goal was startlingly simple: bring a fresh take on Italian pasta to American homes.

First, we took note of American consumers' desire for something honest and real – organic ingredients, cleaner labels and uncomplicated products that fit their modern lifestyle. Next, we worked with the finest producers, growers and makers across Italy, developing authentic Italian foods rooted in quality ingredients and thoughtful craftsmanship.

Why? Because we knew it was time for a change. While so many brands were telling the same old dusty story, we created pasta rooted in Italian tradition, made with organic ingredients and made for real life.

That's The Organic Italian.

Organic Isn't Just a Headline

It's our standard.

We know great pasta starts with thoughtfully sourced ingredients, traditional Italian craftsmanship and a commitment to doing things the right way. And so, everything we do starts with organic Italian wheat. It's what gives our pasta that naturally golden color and perfect al dente bite.

Simple ingredients.
Beautiful flavor.
Nothing unnecessary.

The Art of the Shape

Every pasta shape arrives with its own personality.

Ribbons, both thick and thin, slink through rich sauces like silk. Spirals and twists eagerly gather every last drop, refusing to leave any flavor behind. Ribbed tubes stand ready to catch the chunkiest ragus. And playful bow ties scoop up creamy, cheesy goodness.

Every shape in The Organic Italian collection was selected with intention and purpose — to carry flavor, create balance and make every bite worth savoring.

Made in Naples…Brought to America

Naples is one of the most celebrated pasta-making regions in Italy. And that's where The Organic Italian is made.

Working with experienced pasta makers and trusted partners, we combine generations of Italian expertise with modern production standards to create pasta with authentic flavor and exceptional texture.

It's the best of Italian tradition. Made for a modern American lifestyle.

Bella Italia is on a mission to bring authentic Italian food experiences to new audiences. The Organic Italian represents the next chapter in that journey.
