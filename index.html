About Us

An Organic Beginning

The Organic Italian got its start the way the best dinners do – thoughtfully, hungrily and with way too many people around the table.

First, we took note of American consumers' desire for something honest and real – organic ingredients, cleaner labels and uncomplicated products that fit their modern lifestyle.

Why? Because we knew it was time for a change. We created pasta rooted in Italian tradition, made with organic ingredients and made for real life.

That's The Organic Italian.

Celebrating Organic Moments

Good food brings people together.

The impromptu dinner party. The comfortably crowded table. The second bottle…the third. The friend who stays a little too late. The night that drifts into dawn.

Nothing complicated. Nothing planned. Just great ingredients and even better company.

The Organic Italian was created for those moments.

Organic Isn't Just a Headline

It's our standard.

Everything we do starts with organic Italian wheat — it's what gives our pasta that naturally golden color and perfect al dente bite.

Simple ingredients. Beautiful flavor. Nothing unnecessary.

The Art of the Shape

Every pasta shape arrives with its own personality.

Ribbons slink through rich sauces like silk. Spirals gather every last drop. Ribbed tubes catch the chunkiest ragus. Bow ties scoop up creamy, cheesy goodness.

Every shape was selected with purpose — to carry flavor, create balance, and make every bite worth savoring.

Made in Naples…Brought to America

Naples is one of the most celebrated pasta-making regions in Italy. And that's where The Organic Italian is made.

We combine generations of Italian expertise with modern production standards to create pasta with authentic flavor and exceptional texture.

The best of Italian tradition. Made for a modern American lifestyle.
